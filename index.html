LonelyLessAustralia Decision Aid

Exploring value for money of loneliness support programmes for older adults in Australia

How to use this tool

  1. Choose a loneliness support configuration for older adults (programme type, connection format, contact frequency, delivery mode).
  2. Set the monthly cost per participant, numbers of participants and groups, and the programme horizon.
  3. Optionally adjust for cost-of-living differences across Australian states and territories.
  4. Review endorsement, preference-based benefits, and benefit–cost ratios in the “Results” tab.
  5. Explore population-level implications and scenario comparisons for policy discussions.

The metrics are based on a mixed logit discrete choice model of older Australians’ preferences for loneliness support programmes. WTP and uptake are indicative and should be combined with local judgement and implementation evidence.
